KSA 't Vlinderke

Being a part of this youth movement for many years, I just wanted to make a beautiful and happy site for these magnificent boys and girls that make this youth movement what it is. This is one of the biggest projects I did on my own.

From wireframing, to designing and development, this was a huge challenge. Having a dynamic calendar-system, file-management, media-management, blog-system... you get the picture. By using the low-profile Perch CMS, this site is blazing fast but also very easy to maintain. Please take a look.
